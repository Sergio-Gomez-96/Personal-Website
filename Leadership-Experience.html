​Engineering Students Advisory Board (ESAB) 

President

​ESAB acts as the unifying engineering society for all active engineering societies and select computer science societies at SFSU. The goal of ESAB is to act in the best interests of students and as President I led the following accomplishments:

  • -
    Expanded the amount of internal board members from 4 to 11 people.
  • -
    Hosted the first in person events since the pandemic began.
  • -
    Held weekly internal meetings as well as general meetings with representatives from societies such as ASCE, ASME, SHPE, NSBE, SWE, etc.
  • -
    Successfully planned held a virtual Engineering & Computer Science Career Fair at SFSU.
  • -
    Proactively designated tasks for all internal board members to accomplish multiple goals.
  • -
    Oversaw the planning and funding of the first in person Engineering Showcase and Engineering Graduation since the pandemic started.

​Society of Hispanics Professional Engineers (SHPE) 

President, Vice President

​SHPE is the largest organization of Hispanics in STEM whose goal is to grow the amount of Hispanics in STEM fields. I was president of the student chapter of SHPE at San Francisco State and during my time we were able to accomplish the following:

  • -
    Won the best student chapter of the year award from our National chapter.
  • -
    Hosted multiple companies at two virtual engineering career fairs at SFSU including NASA, Accenture, Kimley Horn, etc.
  • -
    Increased the budget for our society by over 200%.
  • -
    Hosted 12 professional development events over the year and many social events for students.
